---
title: What is this?
---
This book is an attempt by the community to make a learning resource for *anyone* who wishes to learn the Haxe programming language. It assumes no prior programming experience, just a willingness to learn, and by the end you should hopefully have a few different small projects under your belt including a full game.

Be warned however, that this book is unable to teach you *everything*. Learning to program is a lifelong journey that will never truly end. The sole job of this book is to build a strong foundation that you will be able to build on in your future Haxe endeavours.

Sounds good? Lets hop in!
